About Our Pet Adoption Platform

Our Backstory

Berenice and Jessica are two animal lovers currently attending university for computer science who wanted to provide resources to pet lovers looking to adopt, foster, rehome, and donate.

After volunteering at local animal shelters, we saw firsthand how many pets needed homes and how difficult it was to connect them with loving families. We combined our passion for animals with our technical skills to create this platform.

Our Mission

We see so many animals on the streets and are hoping to make a difference for our community. Our mission is to reduce pet homelessness through community adoption events, education, and creating connections between pets and people.

Every animal deserves a loving home, and we're committed to making that process easier for both pets and adopters through our online platform and local partnerships.

How You Can Help

We have lots of available options for you to help us with our mission:

  • Adopt - Give a pet their forever home
  • Foster - Provide temporary care for pets in transition
  • Rehome - Help find new homes for pets you can no longer keep
  • Donate - Support our operations and community programs
  • Volunteer - Assist at events or with daily operations

Any little bit helps in our mission to improve the lives of animals in our community.
