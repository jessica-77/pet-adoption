About Our Pet Adoption Platform

Our Backstory

Berenice and Jessica are two animal lovers currently attending university for computer science. They wanted to provide resources to pet lovers looking to adopt, foster, rehome, volunteer, and/or donate.

We understand not everyone can fully commit to adopting a pet, so we have tried to provide different ways to make an impact to your community. Any help is appreciated and noticed by our team! Thank you for all that you do for our pets.

We see so many pets on the street, scared and in need of a forever home. We understand how difficult it can be to connect them with loving families, so we found the perfect opportunity to combine our passion for animals with our technical skills to create this platform.

Our Mission

Our mission is to reduce pet homelessness through community adoption events, resources, and creating connections between pets and people.

We aim to provide a safe and easy way for people to find their perfect pet, while also helping those pets find their forever homes.

For those who are unable to make bigger commitments of adopting or fostering, are able to make donations of any amount. We also have many volunteer opportunities available for both the youth and those who are 18 and older, who want to help out in their community.

Every animal deserves a loving home, and we're committed to making that process easier for both pets and adopters through our online platform and local partnerships.

How You Can Help

We have lots of available options for you to help us with our mission:

  • Adopt - Give a pet their forever home
  • Foster - Provide temporary care for pets in transition
  • Rehome - Help find new homes for pets you can no longer keep
  • Volunteer - Assist at events or with daily operations
  • Donate - Support our operations and community programs

With your help we can help improve the lives of animals in our community!
